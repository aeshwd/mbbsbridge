Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is NEET mandatory for MBBS in Russia?

Yes, NEET qualification is required for Indian students to be eligible for admission and later register with NMC.

2. Is the MBBS degree from Russia valid in India?

Yes, as long as the university is approved by NMC. After completing MBBS, you must clear the FMGE/NExT exam to practice in India.

3. What is the duration of MBBS in Russia?

The MBBS course in Russia is 6 years, including clinical rotations and internships.

4. What is the medium of instruction?

Most top universities offer MBBS in English medium, especially for Indian and international students.
